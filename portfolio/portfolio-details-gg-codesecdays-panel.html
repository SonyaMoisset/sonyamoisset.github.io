Panel information

  • Title: Imagine a world where software supply chain security is solved
  • Date: 28 June, 2023
  • URL: YouTube

This panel discussion brings together an expert panel of developer and security advocates from Snyk, GitGuardian, Doppler, and Chainguard, with Rachel Stephens from RedMonk moderating as we imagine a world where the challenges of software supply chain security have been solved. A world where developers do not have to worry about the security implications of their software supply chain, where there are no more vulnerabilities in open-source software, and where secrets management is effortless and secure. What would such a world look like?
